Use Case

When you have a topographic printed in paper or have a scanned copy of it and you have to digitalize it in AutoCAD. Topographos will automate the tidious process of having to type one-by-one all the coordinates' numbers from paper to AutoCAD.

How do I use it

Topographos follows 3 very simple steps, upload, fix and import to AutoCAD. By clicking start above Topographos will take you to step one. Each step is explained on the respective page.

Step1: Upload Photo

In the first step Topographos, requires you to upload the image of the coordinates table. You can either browse your computer's files and select an image (not a pdf, more on that later), or take a photo with your mobile device.
